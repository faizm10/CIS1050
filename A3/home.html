Short Info About Me

Hey there! My name is Faiz Mustansar. I'm studying for a Bachelor's degree in Computing with a focus on Computer Science at the University of Guelph. I've been fascinated by computers and technology since I was in 10th grade. That's when I first started learning about computer science. It amazed me how much we can do with computers and how they can change our lives.

My school journey has been full of learning and growing, both personally and academically. I went to Harold M. Brathwaite Secondary School in Brampton, Ontario, where I graduated with a diploma. After that, I decided to go to the University of Guelph to dive deeper into Computer Science. I believe it's the best place for me to learn more about my passion.

Now, I'm almost done with my first year at university. It's been a busy time, trying to keep up with my studies, doing projects on my own, and making sure I stay on the Dean's List. On top of that, I love playing sports and joining clubs. It helps me take a break from studying and do something fun.

This year, I've tried to do a bit of everything. I've spent a lot of time studying and working on my computer skills. But I also made sure to play my favorite sports like soccer and badminton and be a part of different student groups. It's been a great way to meet new friends and learn new things outside of my courses.

I'm excited to see what the next years of university will bring. I want to keep learning more about computer science, work on more interesting projects, and maybe even help teach others what I've learned. It's going to be a lot of hard work, but I can't wait to see where it all leads.
